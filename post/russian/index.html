Grammar:

  • End of word consonants are pronounced like their voiceless counterparts:
    • б п
    • в ф
    • г к
    • д т
    • ж ш
    • з с
  • Consonants are voiceless when followed by other voiceless consonants and the other way around
  • Plurals are built with infinitive + ‘и’/‘ь’
  • ‘что’ is used for alive objects, and ‘кто’ for inanimate objects.
  • Plurals formation:
    • masculine: ending in a consonant or й, ёнок, онок, тель
    • feminine: ending in a, я or ия, ость
    • neuter: ending in o, e, ие or ия
    • other:
      • ending in ь can be either masculine or feminine
      • gender of animate nouns is defined by the natural sex of a person or animal:
        • мальчик is always masculine
        • девочка is always feminine
