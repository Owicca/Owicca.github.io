  • BRE(Basic Regular Expressions):
    • ^, ., [], [^ ], $, *
    • \?, \|, \+, \(\), \{m,n\}
    • \?: \{0,1}\
    • \*: \{0,}\
    • \+: \{1,}\
  • ERE(Extended Regular Expressions):
    • some meta characters do not need escaping: ?, |, +, (), {m,n}

  • pcre:
    • \w(alphanum + “_”): [A-Za-z0-9_]
    • \W(non-word character): [^A-Za-z0-9_]
    • \b(word boundary): (?<=\W)(?=\w)|(?<=\w)(?=\W)
    • \d: [0-9]
    • \D: [^0-9]
    • \s: [ \t\r\n\v\f]
    • \S: [^ \t\r\n\v\f]
