Total immersion

Home All posts Thoughts Papers

Concerning learning a particular subject(sewing, a new language, rope skipping, etc) you MUST immerse yourself fully in the subject’s environment.

If you want to learn to swim:

  • take swimming classes
  • mentally visualise the techniques
  • watch swimming competitions
  • practice breathing patterns when taking a bath
  • watch youtube tutorials

Of je want een ding leren, moet je omring jezelf met delen van die ding’s milieu.

Of je want enn nieuwe taal leren:

  • lees nieuws schrijven in dat taal
  • luister radio in dat taal
  • proberen te praten in dat taal dagelijks

Din experienta de pana acum, o concluzie este clara. Pentru a invata un subiect cel putin la un nivel mediu de proeficienta TREBUIE sa te inconjori cat mai mult(de preferat in totalitate) cu elemente din mediul subiectului.

Pentru a invata o limba:

  • schimba limba computerului in limba noua
  • consuma media in limba noua(podcast-uri, emisiuni tv, radio, muzica, chiar si video)
  • forteaza-te sa folosesti limba in cat mai multe contexte
